About the project

The Focus Digital Archive emerges from work at the Centre for Humanities Research, University of the Western Cape. This demo is a student built template to show how new users can approach the archive and learn its navigation patterns.

Goals

  • Lower the barrier for first time users, explain how search and browse work.
  • Provide example case studies for teaching and outreach.
  • Offer a neutral space to record testing notes and improvements.
